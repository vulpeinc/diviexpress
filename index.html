International Vehicle Sourcing and Import Coordination

Divi Express LLC is a company specialized in the sale, importation, and commercialization of high-quality vehicles, offering clients a reliable, accessible, and transparent solution to acquire vehicles that meet their needs and expectations.

We focus on sourcing modern, efficient vehicles in excellent condition, carefully selected to meet established standards of performance, safety, and comfort.

Company Overview

Divi Express LLC operates in the international vehicle trade with a structured and compliance-oriented business model. Our activities include vehicle sourcing, import coordination, and commercial support throughout the sales process.

By working exclusively with trusted international suppliers, we are able to offer competitive pricing and access to vehicle models, configurations, and features that are often not readily available in local markets.

Our Services

Our services include:

  • Vehicle sourcing through approved international suppliers
  • Coordination of the vehicle importation process
  • Management of required documentation and regulatory procedures
  • Sales support and organized delivery to the client

All services are provided in accordance with standard commercial and trade practices.

End-to-End Process

Our service extends beyond vehicle sales.

We accompany clients throughout the entire process, beginning with an initial consultation to identify the most suitable vehicle based on preferences and budget. This is followed by full coordination of importation, documentation, regulatory compliance, and final delivery.

Each stage is conducted using structured procedures, clear communication, and adherence to applicable regulations.

Transparency and Compliance

Transparency, responsibility, and reliability are core principles of Divi Express LLC.

We provide clear and accurate information regarding vehicle condition, origin, specifications, and transaction structure, allowing clients to make informed decisions with confidence.

Our operations are designed to ensure compliance, proper recordkeeping, and ethical business conduct at all times.

Divi Express LLC does not provide vehicle financing, credit services, or act as a lender. All transactions are conducted as commercial vehicle sourcing and import coordination services.
